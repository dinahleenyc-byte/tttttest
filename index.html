Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about dorm design, shopping, and move-in day.

Getting Started

When should we start shopping for dorm items?
We recommend starting in June for fall move-in. This gives you time to research, compare prices, and avoid the August rush when popular items sell out.

How do we know what size bedding to buy?
Most dorms use Twin XL mattresses (80" long). Always check your specific housing website, but Twin XL is the safest standard bet.

Design & Style

How can we make a small dorm room feel bigger?
Use light colors, mirrors, vertical storage, and multi-functional furniture. Our "Small Space" design collection specifically addresses this challenge.

Can we use command strips on dorm walls?
Most dorms allow Command strips and hooks, but check your housing agreement. We recommend the heavy-duty velcro strips for anything over 5 pounds.

Shopping & Budget

What's a realistic budget for dorm decorating?
Most families spend between $500-$1,500 total, depending on whether you need electronics and how much storage is required. Our budget breakdowns help prioritize essentials.
